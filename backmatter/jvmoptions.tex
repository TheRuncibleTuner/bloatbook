\chapter{\jre Options Related to Memory}
\label{chapter:jre-options}

\paragraph{Perm space (\oracle only)}. There are JVM parameters to adjust
the perm space size. \code{-XX:PermSize=128m} sets perm size to 128 megabytes,
and \code{-XX:MaxPermSize=512m} sets the maximum perm size to 512 megabytes.

\paragraph{Sharing pool for \class{Integers} (\oracle and \ibm \jres)}. You can
change the upper range of the built-in \class{Integer} sharing pool by setting 
\code{-XX:AutoBoxCacheMax=size}. You can only extend the range upward; the upper
limit is always a minimum of 127.
